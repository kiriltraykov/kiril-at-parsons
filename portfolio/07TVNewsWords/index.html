News Media Language

Hello! My name is Vessela and I am about to read the news of the hour!

The script I have has a lot of words, but they could be predictable.

Let's pick one! Shuffle the list using the buttons to the left.

Can you guess how often I would say ?

Rare = 1-9 times per million words, Sometimes = 10-49 times, Frequently = 50-99 times, Very Often = 100+ times

Shuffle again!

When done playing game, you can see all answers here
Data source: Corpus of Contemporary American English (COCA) for spoken word frequency by news TV stations from Brigham Young University, search engine available at: http://corpus.byu.edu/coca/
